ABOUT US

LUKE ALTIERI, B.B.A in Management Information Systems
Owner

Luke founded FixIT to provide IT support that is trustworthy, timely, and thorough.

From an early age, Luke had a knack for all things tech related. Whether it was building his first computer, setting up his parents' home WiFi network, or installing an in-home theater system, he always knew that his love for technology would be lifelong. After years of being approached by family and friends for help with their technology needs, a light bulb went off and FixIT was born.

Over the past decade, Luke has helped countless clients with a variety of technology goals. He uses a unique combination of deep industry expertise, hard work ethic, and a genuine desire to help others to forge long term partnerships. One of Luke's core values is that if you do a job, make sure it is done the right way. He strives to go above and beyond for each and every client.
